Why Manganese?

Manganese, element number 25, is essential to human health, essential to the health of the planet through its role in photosynthesis, and essential to industry as a strengthener of steel. Its compounds work artistic magic too, one as glassmakers' soap to make glass colorless and one as manganese violet to paint the world purple. The element thus symbolizes the synergy between the sciences and the arts.
